Design notes — confirm or change
  • Inline expand, not a modal. The 💬 Notes button toggles a panel under the step. Count badge shows how many notes the viewer can see.
  • Private toggle (coach/supervisor only) — a private note is visible only to its author. Teachers viewing their own profile never see private notes and don't get the toggle. Mirrors Grow's "Make Private."
  • Who can post: the teacher (on their own profile), their supervisor chain, admins, content leads. Same as who can see the profile.
  • No file attachments in v1. Grow has "Attach File" — we can add it later via the existing FileDrop component if you want, but v1 is text only.
  • No "Show Shared / Show Private" filter (Grow has it). With private-to-author-only, there's nothing to filter — you only ever see shared notes + your own private ones. Keeps it simpler.
  • Storage: new action_step_notes table (id, action_step_id, author_email, body, is_private, created_at). New endpoints: GET notes for a step, POST a note.
  • Mastered steps: notes stay readable on mastered/archived steps (the thread is the coaching record), but the composer is hidden once a step is mastered. Confirm?
