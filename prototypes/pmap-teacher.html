▶ Descriptors
1 — Needs Improvement: Very few or no students complete instructional tasks, volunteer responses and/or ask appropriate questions. Students do not execute transitions, routines and procedures in an orderly manner. Students are left without work for a significant portion of the class period.

2 — Emerging: Some students complete instructional tasks. Students execute transitions only some of the time and/or require substantial direction. Students are idle for one or two minutes at a time.

3 — Developing: Most students complete instructional tasks and demonstrate understanding of expectations. Transitions mostly orderly, may require some direction. Students idle for short periods (<1 min).

4 — Proficient: All or almost all students complete tasks and follow expectations. Transitions orderly with minimal direction. Quick pace from start to finish. Students who finish early engage in meaningful learning.

5 — Exemplary: Level 4 met + students self-direct routines requiring no direction. Students hold each other accountable for behavioral expectations.
