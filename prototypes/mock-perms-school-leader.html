What's different
  • Blue banner at top makes the scope explicit ("you can compare, but only drill into your own school")
  • Own school cell: orange border + "YOUR SCHOOL" badge + clickable
  • Other-school cells: dimmed (~55% opacity) + lock icon + non-clickable + tooltip on hover
  • Same hero foot treatment
  • All comparison numbers stay visible — that's the value of being on the Network page
Open questions
  • Is the orange border + "YOUR SCHOOL" badge enough emphasis, or do we want the own-school cell visually larger?
  • Lock icon (🔒) on disabled cells — keep, drop, or replace with something else?
  • Banner text — does "you can compare across schools but only drill into your own" feel right, or simpler ("Phillis Wheatley · view-only on other schools")?
  • If a school leader pastes another school's drill-down URL directly, should we redirect them to their own school's drill-down, or show a friendly 403?
Backend changes (already specified in PERMISSIONS.md)
  • New auth.get_user_school_scope(user) helper — returns 'admin', a school name, or None
  • /api/network/drilldown forces school param to user's school for school-scoped users
  • /api/auth/status returns the scope so frontend can render dimmed cells correctly
