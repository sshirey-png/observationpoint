### School Leader

`school_leader`

Sees Network-level trends and per-school comparisons. Can only click
INTO their own school for drill-downs and the school deep-dive page.

**Titles (keyword match):** `principal`, `assistant principal`, `dean`, `director of culture`

### Supervisor

`supervisor`

Anyone with at least one direct report. Sees their recursive downline.
Not school-scoped тАФ a network-team supervisor sees their reports
regardless of school.

**Rule:** at least one row in staff where supervisor_email = user.email AND is_active

### Self-only

`self_only`

Active staff with no direct reports and no admin/content-lead/school-leader
title. Sees their own profile, action steps, self-reflections, etc.

**Rule:** active staff who don't match any tier above
