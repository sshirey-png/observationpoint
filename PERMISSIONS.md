## Tiers

### Admin

`admin`

Broad FEATURE access (Network page, drill-downs, impersonation, the
permissions matrix). But per-person DATA scope splits in two — this
mirrors the Supervisor Dashboard model so the structure is repeatable:

  • HR function (CPO + everything in HR_TEAM_TITLES, which includes the
    CEO) → can open ANY staff member's record, PMAPs included. These
    are the only "see everyone" accounts. Helper: auth.is_hr_admin().

  • Other C-Team / ExDirs (CXO, CAO, COO, CSAO, ExDir of Teach and
    Learn, etc.) → see their recursive supervisor downline only, same
    as any supervisor. A Chief sees their own org branch — NOT a peer
    Chief's evaluation. (The CEO's downline is everyone, so she sees
    everything either way.)

"Whose record can I open" = check_access(); "all-staff list" =
get_accessible_emails(). Both key off is_hr_admin, not is_admin.

**Rule:** FEATURE access: job_title matches C_TEAM_KEYWORDS (Chief, ExDir) OR
is in HR_TEAM_TITLES.
ALL-STAFF data access: job_title == 'Chief People Officer' OR in
HR_TEAM_TITLES (is_hr_admin). Everyone else with this tier sees only
their recursive downline.

**Titles (keyword match):** `Chief`, `ExDir`

- Chief Executive Officer
- Chief HR Officer
- Manager, HR
- Manager Payroll
- Manager - Benefits
- Talent Operations Manager
- Recruitment Manager

### School Leader

`school_leader`

Building leadership. Sees Network-level trends and per-school comparisons,
but can only drill INTO their own school's aggregate data (and the school
deep-dive page). Individual staff profiles follow the recursive supervisor
chain — same as the Supervisor Dashboard — NOT blanket "everyone at my
building." If a teacher isn't reachable through the chain, the fix is the
data (set supervisor_email), not a broader rule.

Membership is NOT a hand-maintained title list — it's whatever the warehouse
classifies as Leadership in staff_master_list_with_function's Job_Function
CASE (grow-observations/staff_master_list_with_function.sql). Today that's
Principal / Asst Principal / Dean of Students / School Director / Manager
School Ops / SPED Coordinator / RTI Coordinator (plus anyone whose Function
is 'Emp School Leaders' or 'Emp AP - Deans'). NOT Asst Dean (→ Support),
NOT Dir of Culture (→ Network — it's a network Schools-Team role).

**Rule:** job_function == 'Leadership' (from staff_master_list_with_function) AND school is a real school

### Supervisor

`supervisor`

Anyone with at least one direct report. Sees their recursive downline.
Not school-scoped — a network-team supervisor sees their reports
regardless of school.

**Rule:** at least one row in staff where supervisor_email = user.email AND is_active

### Self-only

`self_only`

Active staff with no direct reports and no admin/content-lead/school-leader
title. Sees their own profile, action steps, self-reflections, etc.

**Rule:** active staff who don't match any tier above
